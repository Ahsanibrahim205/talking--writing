Ways of practicing talking and writing

  • Keep a daily diary of your thoughts and feelings.
  • Identify situations that cause problems and why
  • Talk through any issues with a close colleague.
  • Check your perception of the situation with other people and how they would deal with things.
  • Reward yourself for writing something every day for a week and then maybe extend the period to a month.
